How the Calculator Calculates

The Webnovel Earnings Calculator uses the following formula to calculate your earnings:

Earnings = ((Subs * Coin Price) + Privilege Coins) * 0.02 * 0.28

Where:

  • Subs = The number of subscriptions your novel has. You can see it in inkstone dashboard.
  • Coin Price = The coin price of a chapter based on the number of words. (see the image for details)
  • Privilege Coins = The number of Privilege Coins you earned from readers unlocking your privilege tiers. You can see it on inkstone -> promote -> privelage.
